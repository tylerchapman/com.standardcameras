The Standard 4x5

Specs

  • Accepts Linhof style lens boards
  • Approximately 100mm to 300mm lenses accepted*
  • Front Standard Movements: Rise, Shift, Swing and Tilt
  • Rear Standard Movements: Shift and Swing
  • Only 2 pounds (0.91kg)
  • Bellows available in Black and Gray
  • 3d Printed using a fully recyclable PETG plastic filament
  • Anodized aluminum components and Stainless Steel hardware
  • Ground glass can be quickly removed to use various graflok backs
  • Lens is not included
About

The Standard 4x5 is a simple, lightweight and affordable 4x5 view camera - available fully assembled or as a DIY kit!
